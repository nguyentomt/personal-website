Project: DoGooder App v.1


What: Harness Crowdfunding for local construction projects, public spaces; a gov't revenue source; contract with local/state/federal govts to get microdonations from wide net of citizens for projects

Pitch:
Harness the power of microdonations
How many people in the US?
330-350 million, officially. More, realistically.
Imagine if 1% of the USA donated 1 cent?
$35,000.
Direct $ to local projects

Imagine doing that 1 time. Amazing, great. Imagine it's on-going, continuous.

Imagine an app that can harness the power of crowdfunding and microdonations for local construction projects to improve public spaces, while also helping its users save time, money, and energy. Similar to Kickstarter, projects will not receive funding unless the goal is reached.

Incentives for users: Tax deductible charitable donations, transparency in where their money goes, feeling of contributing to greater causes, social edification, Bronze-Silver-Gold tier sponsorships, notification when project breaks ground/is completed, name credit in digital plaque, name credit in T-shirt; crowdsourced best advice for saving Time, Energy, and Money, ability to upvote projects/advices, Crowdsourced wisdom attributed to fellow DoGooders from ${donor.location.state}.

Offer: Price comparisons for products from local Walmart/Target/Costco/7-11/Amazon; Practical/helpful advice, lifehacks;

More: Tailor some advice to region, user preferences
